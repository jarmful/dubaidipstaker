KYC requirements

OKX

  • Difficulty: Easy–medium.
  • Typical time: 10–30 minutes for basic; up to 24 hours for full.
  • Documents: Passport or national ID, selfie, sometimes proof of address (utility bill or bank statement).

Crypto.com

  • Difficulty: Easy.
  • Typical time: 10–20 minutes; some cases up to 1 business day.
  • Documents: Passport or ID, selfie, proof of address if requested.

Kraken

  • Difficulty: Medium.
  • Typical time: 20–30 minutes; up to a few days for higher limits.
  • Documents: Passport or ID, selfie, proof of residence (utility bill, bank statement).

Make sure to confirm the latest KYC rules and supported countries on each platform’s official website before applying.
